📦 How to Build Your Site

  1. Pick a category or styling topic.
  2. Copy a prompt and paste it into your AI tool of choice.
  3. Replace the blanks with your specific design choices (colors, layout, features).
  4. Ask the AI to split the output into separate files: index.html, styles.css, and script.js.
  5. Download the files and preview them locally or publish using a tool like Vercel, Netlify, or GitHub Pages.
